Vikings Strategy Notes

General:
- Prioritize sustained rally uptime.
- Use 2–3 alternating rally leaders to minimize downtime.
- Run lvl 20+ Vikings for best XP and gear chest ratio.

Hero Use:
- Use strongest hero for rally lead.
- Keep backup hero for continuous rallies.

Optimization:
- Avoid splitting damage across too many targets.
- Assist weaker members by timing rallies they can join.

Tone:
- Tactical but lighthearted: “Vikings are just BT with boats.”
